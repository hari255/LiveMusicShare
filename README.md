# LiveMusicShare 🎧

## Discover the soundtrack around you.

LiveMusicShare is a real-time music discovery platform that helps people discover songs through what others are listening to nearby.

Instead of traditional recommendation algorithms, LiveMusicShare uses real-world listening activity to surface music trends, hidden gems, and local music culture.

---

# Vision

Music discovery should feel human.

People discover amazing songs from:
- friends
- DJs
- concerts
- random moments

LiveMusicShare brings that experience into a digital space.
